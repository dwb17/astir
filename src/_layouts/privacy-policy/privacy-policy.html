Our goal

To protect and process any personal information provided to us, with lawful, fair and transparent practices in relation to the data subject.

Regulatory compliance

Processing and protection of personal data that are collected in this website, are governed by the terms of the current Privacy Policy and legislation in force, regarding legal rights of data subjects.

Data that we collect and how we use it

Every visitor may provide personal information such as: name, surname, e-mail, phone number, address. By submitting these data, visitors give their express and unreserved consent to the processing of these data and their transfer to third parties as described below. Personal data are stored, distributed and processed by our trained staff, depending on the nature of the enquiry. Access to personal data is only given to authorized personnel, as appropriate and depending on the purpose of the processing.

Our employees treat these data with confidentiality, due diligence and consideration, in accordance with current legislation in force.

Processing includes the following actions: collection, recording, organizing, structuring, storing, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, combination or alignment, restriction, erasure, destruction.

Data distribution

The recipients of personal data that the Company is entitled to notify under certain occasions such as contract, relevant legislation, judicial and regulatory decision may be public and independent administrative authorities, services, judicial authorities as well as civil servants. In any other case, the Company is committed that any stored personal data will not be disclosed, sold, exchanged or granted. You are advised not to send any personal data through unsafe means. A secure communication channel via ‘Contact Us’ filling form is provided at this website.

Visitor Rights – Communication

Every data subject maintains the right of access to his personal data held by the Company and to know whether his personal data are or have been processed, the purpose of their processing, the recipients and the progress of the processing. In any case, this person might ask for a copy and/or declare a change, erasure, limit/restrict processing, oppose the processing as well as the portability of such data. For that purpose, every person should contact the Company by sending an e-mail to the following address: privacy@vitogiannis.gr or communicating with the Data Protection Officer at d.kriebardis@vitogiannis.gr.

The Company will make sure that this request will be fulfilled at the earliest convenience. You also have the right to make a complaint to the Local supervisory authority.

Visitors may withdraw acceptance of a consent to process their personal data if such data are no longer necessary in relation to the purpose for which have been submitted and are not necessary for further retention due to legal or other obligations of the Company.

Data Retention

We retain your Personal Information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of processing, based on your request unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Cookies

To ensure the effectiveness of this website, data related to the electronic equipment used for access, are recorded automatically. These data include information like the IP address of the visitor, as well as data from “cookies” that are sent and installed on your computer. The “cookies” do not have access to your computer nor are they aware of any document or file on your computer and they provide us with the ability to detect the manner of navigation of the visitors on our website as well as information on the pages visited and the choices made during the visit. Information like the number and the frequency of visits can also be recorded to improve characteristics like the content and the quality of our website.

If you want to delete any cookies that may already be on your computer or device, please refer to the instructions for your file management software to locate the file or directory that stores cookies. If you want to stop cookies being stored on your computer in future visits, please refer to your browser manufacturer’s instructions by clicking “Help” in your browser menu. Further information on cookies is available at www.allaboutcookies.org. By deleting our cookies or disabling future cookies you may not be able to access certain areas or features of our website.

Children

The use of this website is not addressed to children therefore, we do not collect personal information from children under the age of eighteen (18). In case, any submission from a visitor under the age of eighteen (18) in the website is observed, data will be deleted.
